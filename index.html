Anna Lee

“Freshman Business Major”

Anna is a freshman business major, and just moved to a new city. She’s planning to register to vote at school, but has no idea how to start figuring out who her new representatives are.

Goals

  • Graduate with a business degree
  • Figure out who represents her in her new home
  • Understand local policy

Frustrations

  • Completely new to the area, doesn’t know where to begin
  • Not too versed in how government works to begin with (“I want to be engaged, but I don’t even know where to start.”)
  • Can’t find info on local issues
